wet shave

STEP 1

Prep brush by soaking in warm water for a minute or so.

STEP 2

Prep Face by applying a warm damp cloth to your face for roughly 30 seconds.

STEP 3

Shake water off brush. Lather soap by moving the brush in a circular motion in the soap container

STEP 4

Apply lather by using the brush in a circular motion to draw facial hair outward away from it's roots.

STEP 5

Shave with the grain at a 30-45 degree angle - using the weight of the razor to shave.

STEP 6

Rinse off face with cold water and apply aftershave or balm to reduce irritation and moisturize skin.
